MASS COMMUNICATION DEFINED

What is communication?

Acoording to

Harold Lasswell

of 1948, communication must be able to answer these five questions:

1. WHO: the communicator/ sender

2. SAYS WHAT: the message

3. IN WHICH CHANNEL: the medium used

4. TO WHOM: the audience/ receiver

5. WITH WHAT EFFECT: the outcome, impact, or result

Therefore, comuunication can be defined as the process of disseminating information, idea, opinion through a seder to a receiver via a specific channel .

What is mass communication?

Mass communication can be defined as the process of disseminating information, idea, opinion etc to a large, heterogenious, and diverse audience through an electro-magnetic spectrum.
